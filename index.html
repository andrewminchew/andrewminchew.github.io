Rather than try to make this website do all the things, I thought I'd use it as a hub to point to my other web spots:

  • QuikCal - I've long been a fan of Dsri Seah's Compact Calendar (a whole year on a single page with room for notes and no layout jankiness between months). I thought it might be cool to turn this idea into a little bit of web that would generate 365 days before and after today and lay it out nice and tight, so I talked it over with ChatGPT and here we are.
  • MicroJournal - I found myself not journaling because I didn't have time, while also constantly thinking about re-engaging on social media just to have somewhere to share a quick dumb thought and get the satisfaction of seeing my own words in the web browser, so I made this little micro-journal project with ChatGPT's help. Basically you input to the field at the bottom and it stores a record in the local storage on your device. If you want to pull your records for use elsewhere, you can export as CSV from the settings.
  • Blog - I have fond memories of blogging from way back in the wordpress & blogger days. I enjoyed a creative writing class in college. I have a tendency to undercut the value of my own opinions. Slowly building back up to some kind of publishing momentum.
  • LinkedIn - I've slowly but surely tried to deactivate and delete my various social media accounts because I don't think they're doing anyone any good, but LinkedIn feels important because work and jobs.
  • Metaverse studio - I haven't given up on the Metaverse just yet and so far, this has been one of the more practical applications I've found. It's somewhere between Fortnite and Zoom and I still enjoy it when visitors drop in. If I'm available, when I get notified of a guest, I'll definitely pop in. Still noodling on ways to make the space more interesting.

I have every intention of spiffing this place up a bit and adding more links in the future. Just trying to take one step at a time.
